# A Rustling Grass / 微风草丛

当前版本 / Current Version: `0.9.0`

## 中文简介
《微风草丛》是一款以文字氛围、宠物养成与章节冒险为核心的回合制 RPG。

你会从微风村出发，选择自己的职业与初始伙伴，走进摇曳作响的草丛、逐步解锁更深处的区域，在一次次遭遇、分支选择、战斗与委托中，把一段原本安静的乡野故事推向更大的冒险。

这并不是一个只靠数值推进的游戏。你会遇到能并肩而行的人，也会面对是否独自前进的选择；你能收服野生宠物、翻开怪兽图鉴、完成村庄里的委托、积攒风纹币、购买补给，并在逐步成形的世界里看见属于这段旅程的轮廓。

如果你喜欢：
- 轻小说式的文字冒险氛围
- 宠物收集、培养与捕获机制
- 回合制战斗与队伍分支成长
- 带有章节推进感的村庄探索

那么《微风草丛》就是一个正在不断长大的原型世界。

当前版本已包含：
- 4 个职业：战士、弓兵、魔法师、牧师
- 初始宠物选择：火尾狐、水泡蛙、叶芽兽
- 回合制战斗、技能与 MP 消耗
- 单人 / 双人队伍分支
- 宠物捕获、稀有度与怪兽图鉴
- 村庄浏览、NPC 对话、委托任务、商店与货币系统
- 多存档位与第一章基础流程

## English Overview
A Rustling Grass is a text-driven turn-based RPG built around atmosphere, creature collecting, and chapter-based adventure.

You begin in Breeze Village, choose your class and starter companion, and step into rustling grasslands and deeper wild zones. Through encounters, branching choices, battles, village requests, and party decisions, a quiet rural beginning gradually opens into a larger journey.

This is not just a numbers-first RPG. It is also about tone, companionship, choice, and discovery: recruiting allies, deciding whether to travel alone, capturing wild creatures, filling out the monster dex, earning currency, buying supplies, and watching the world slowly take shape as Chapter One unfolds.

If you enjoy:
- text-heavy adventure with a light novel feel
- creature capture and collection systems
- turn-based combat with party route choices
- village exploration tied to story progression

then A Rustling Grass is meant to feel like a growing prototype world with clear ambitions.

The current build includes:
- 4 player classes: Warrior, Archer, Mage, Priest
- 3 starter companions: Firetail Fox, Bubble Frog, Sprout Beast
- Turn-based combat with skills and MP costs
- Solo / duo party route choices
- Creature capture, rarity tiers, and a monster dex
- Village exploration, NPC dialogue, side tasks, shops, and currency
- Multiple save slots and Chapter One progression
